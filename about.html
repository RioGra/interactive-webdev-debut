    Welcome to FLORAL PHENOMENA

    Where you will delve into The Floral Phenomena of Perfume.

    Exploring the enchanting world of botanical perfumery.

    Perfume has captivated humanity for centuries.

    The allure of floral fragrances

    has a profound impact on our emotions, memories and connection with others.

    This immersive experience will guide you through the history

    and art of creating natural perfumes using aromatic plants.

    Enchanted by the scents of blooming flowers in her grandmothers garden.

    A fascination with the art of scent became a lifelong love affair.

    Join us as we trace the origin and evolution of perfume

    and fragrance design at our exclusive perfume workshop.
