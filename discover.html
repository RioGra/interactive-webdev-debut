The Art of Botanical Perfume Creation

Prepare to Embark on a Fragrant Journey that will Awaken your Senses

Inspire Your Creativity in a Collaborative Atmosphere !

Workshop Details

Date

Friday 21st March 2025

Time

3pm-7pm

Duration

4 Hours

Location

The Nash Conservatory,The Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew, London TW9 3AE

Price

£85 per participant

Workshop Description

Participants will learn about the origins and history of perfumery, the structure of scents and the secret behind blending essential oils. Throughout the workshop, you will engage in hands-on activities to craft your own unique 10ml natural perfume rollerball blend to take home.

This is a perfect opportunity for both beginners and seasoned enthusiasts to expand their knowledge and skills in the art of fragrance creation.

Your Host and Speaker

The Perfume Mistress

Inspired by her trip to Kazanlak in Bulgaria to learn about their rose oil production and the luxurious experience of laying on a bed of 300,000 roses. The perfume mistress will present to you an immersive perfume masterclass this January. This workshop takes place at the stunning Nash Conservatory in the beautiful surrounds of The Royal Bontanic Gardens at Kew, London. This workshop is designed for both beginners and experienced individuals who want to explore the art of botanical perfumery.
